Our story

Food is the most honest
form of love.

Eeet was born from a simple obsession: that the best meals are the ones you remember not just for what you tasted, but for how they made you feel. We source every ingredient from farms we trust, chefs we believe in, and traditions we respect.

Our menu changes with the seasons — not because it's trendy, but because real food should taste like where and when it came from. Pull up a chair. Stay a while.

We opened our doors in 2024 with one conviction: that a neighbourhood bistro should feel like the most welcoming room you've ever been in. Not precious, not performative — just honest, generous, and alive.

"Cook with memory,
eat with intention."

Local roots
Every supplier is within 200km. We know their names and visit their farms.
Seasonal always
The menu rotates four times a year. What's ripe is what's on the plate.
No shortcuts
Stocks are made overnight. Bread is baked every morning. Some things just take time.
